01  Time Period of Production
The Total Incubation and Hatching Process Period is 21 days, Out of which 18 days are meant for Incubation and 3 days for Hatching Process. Accross this 21 days, the Essential parameters had to be maintained precisely inorder to achieve good production rate.
02  Essential Parameters
Temperature, Humidity, CO2 Gas Content are the mandatory parameters that are to be monitored more carefully during the process of Incubation and Hatching.
03  Effect in Variation
Due to the variation in any of the incubation parameters, the production of eggs are ultimately affected. Lack of proper temperature - 21% eggs wasted, Variation of 0.2% of CO2 - 5% eggs are wasted, Insufficient oxygen (0.1%) - 0.5% eggs are wasted.
